# Ever Launcher

> **Your phone, focused.**

Ever Launcher is a minimalist Android home screen replacement designed to reduce digital distraction and help you stay intentional.

## What is Ever Launcher?

- **Focus Modes** — Your home screen automatically adapts based on the time of day (Focus, Personal, Wind Down).
- **Mindful Gates** — Add intentional friction before opening distracting apps: breathing pause, intention prompt, or delay.
- **On-Device Analytics** — Track your daily Focus Score, screen time, and streaks. All data stays on your device. No cloud, no tracking.
- **Fully Offline** — No ads, no analytics SDKs, no subscriptions, no backend servers.

## Availability

Ever Launcher is available on the Google Play Store as a one-time paid app.
