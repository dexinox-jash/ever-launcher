# Feature Graphic Specifications

## Required Dimensions
- **Width:** 1024 px
- **Height:** 500 px
- **Format:** PNG or JPG (PNG recommended for quality)
- **Max file size:** 1 MB

## Design Direction

### Suggested Layout
1. **Left 60%:** Large, clean typography
   - Headline: "Your phone, focused."
   - Subheadline: "A minimalist launcher for intentional living."
2. **Right 40%:** Visual element
   - A subtle phone mockup showing the Ever Launcher home screen (time + short app list)
   - Or an abstract circular focus-ring graphic (inspired by the Focus Score ring UI)

### Color Palette
Use colors from the app's theme for consistency:

| Theme | Background | Accent | Text Primary |
|-------|------------|--------|--------------|
| Light | #F5F5F0 | #2D5F2D | #111111 |
| Dark | #1A1A1C | #C8FF00 | #F0F0F0 |

**Recommendation:** Create two versions:
1. Light version (for Play Store web desktop viewers)
2. Dark version (optional, for A/B testing)

The default Play Store listing should use the **Light version** as the primary feature graphic because it reads better on the dark Play Store web background.

### Typography
- Primary font: Serif (use Playfair Display or similar)
- Secondary font: Clean sans-serif (Inter or Roboto)
- Keep text large — it will be viewed as a thumbnail

### Tools
- **Canva** (free): Search "Google Play feature graphic" template
- **Figma** (free): 1024×500 frame, export as PNG
- **Photoshop / GIMP**: For more advanced compositing

### What NOT to do
- Do not include the Google Play logo
- Do not include device brand logos (Samsung, Pixel, etc.)
- Do not make text smaller than 48 px at 1024×500 resolution
- Do not use stock photos of people looking at phones (looks generic)
